A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the largest terrestrial planet. Unlike other planets in the solar system that are named after classic deities the Earth's name comes from the Anglo-Saxon word erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approimately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
